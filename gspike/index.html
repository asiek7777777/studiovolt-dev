GSpike

G-Force Meter for iPhone & Apple Watch

App Privacy Policy Terms of Service

GSpike is a real-time G-force meter that measures acceleration forces using your device's built-in accelerometer. Record sessions, track your peak and average G-forces, and review your history — all on your iPhone and Apple Watch.

Perfect for rollercoasters, track days, go-karting, drifting, motorsport, skiing, mountain biking, and any activity where you want to know the forces you're pulling.
