Flight Metrics records your flights from a passenger perspective. Track speed, altitude, G-force, and route on a map — all using your phone's built-in sensors.

  • Real-time GPS tracking with route visualization on map
  • Speed, altitude, and distance measurements
  • G-force logging via accelerometer
  • Flight phase detection, taxi, takeoff, cruise, landing
  • Post-flight summary with stats
  • Works in airplane mode, GPS only, no internet needed
  • Made in Switzerland
