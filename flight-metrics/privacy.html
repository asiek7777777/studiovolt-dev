Privacy Policy — Flight Metrics

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Flight Metrics handles your data.

The Short Version

  • All your flight data stays on your device
  • We do not collect personal data
  • There are no accounts, no logins, no tracking
  • We do not sell or share any data

1. Your Data Stays on Your Device

Flight Metrics records flight data (speed, altitude, route, G-force) using your device's built-in GPS and sensors. All recorded data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers that receive your data.

There are no user accounts, no logins, and no cloud storage.

2. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or any form of user tracking.

3. Sensor Data

During a flight recording, the app reads data from your device's sensors:

  • GPS (location, speed, heading, altitude)
  • Barometer (atmospheric pressure)
  • Accelerometer (G-force)

This data is used solely to display your flight metrics and is stored only on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services for map display and subscription management. These services may process minimal, anonymous technical data (such as map tile requests or purchase receipts) according to their own privacy policies. No personal data or flight recordings are shared with these services.

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive your payment details.

5. Data Retention

Your flight recordings and app settings remain on your device until you delete them in the app or uninstall the app. We do not retain any data on our servers.

6. Your Rights

Under Swiss data protection law (nDSG/FADP), EU GDPR, and other applicable laws:

  • You can view all your recorded data in the app
  • You can delete individual flights or all data at any time
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

7. Children

Flight Metrics is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date will reflect any changes.
